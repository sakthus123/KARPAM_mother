KARPAM Antenatal Safety Check (Phase 0). A self-check aid for women with kidney disease or a kidney transplant in pregnancy. It is alert-only: it never shows a risk score and never tells you that you are “fine” or “low risk”.

This tool does not contact the hospital. Entering a reading or ticking a warning sign does not notify your care team. If it tells you to call or attend, you must call or attend yourself.

An adjunct to — never a replacement for — your appointments and clinical care. Not a registered medical device. Tamil text requires native-speaker verification before patient release.

Draft — for internal review and validation only. Not for patient use until obstetric sign-off of the thresholds and danger-signs, Tamil validation, and IEC approval are complete, and the emergency contact number above has been set.
